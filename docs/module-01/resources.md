# Module 1 Resources

## Books {#books}

### Core Robotics
1. **"Introduction to Robotics: Mechanics and Control"** by John J. Craig
   - Classic textbook covering fundamentals
   - Excellent for kinematics and dynamics

2. **"Probabilistic Robotics"** by Thrun, Burgard, and Fox
   - Comprehensive coverage of probabilistic methods
   - Essential for modern robotics

3. **"Modern Robotics: Mechanics, Planning, and Control"** by Lynch and Park
   - Modern approach to robotics theory
   - Free online version available

### AI and Machine Learning
1. **"Deep Learning"** by Goodfellow, Bengio, and Courville
   - Comprehensive introduction to deep learning
   - Available online for free

2. **"Reinforcement Learning: An Introduction"** by Sutton and Barto
   - The definitive RL textbook
   - Second edition includes modern advances

## Online Courses {#courses}

### Robotics
1. **Coursera - Modern Robotics: Mechanics, Planning, and Control**
   - Northwestern University specialization
   - 6 courses covering key robotics topics

2. **Udacity - Robotics Software Engineer**
   - Nanodegree program
   - Hands-on projects with real robots

3. **edX - Robotics Fundamentals**
   - University of Pennsylvania
   - Introduction to core concepts

### AI/ML
1. **Coursera - Machine Learning**
   - Andrew Ng's classic course
   - Great foundation for ML

2. **Fast.ai - Practical Deep Learning for Coders**
   - Free, practical approach
   - Top-down methodology

## Tools and Frameworks

### Simulation
1. **Gazebo**
   - 3D robotics simulator
   - Integrates with ROS

2. **Webots**
   - Professional robot simulator
   - Free for research and education

3. **MuJoCo**
   - High-performance physics engine
   - Recent open source release

### Programming
1. **Python**
   - NumPy, SciPy for mathematical computing
   - PyTorch/TensorFlow for ML

2. **C++**
   - ROS (Robot Operating System)
   - Performance-critical applications

## Competitions

1. **RoboCup**
   - International robotics competitions
   - Various leagues and categories

2. **DARPA Robotics Challenge**
   - Advanced robotics tasks
   - Pushes the boundaries of what's possible

3. **FIRST Robotics**
   - High school competitions
   - Great for beginners
